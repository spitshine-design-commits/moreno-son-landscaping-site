About Us

Family-owned. Quality-driven. Northeast Ohio proud.

Our Story

Before Moreno & Son Landscaping ever had a name, Victor Moreno was already doing the work — helping friends, cousins, and uncles with landscaping and outdoor projects whenever they needed a hand. Over the years, he built real knowledge and expertise, and in 2016, he decided to turn that experience into something of his own.

When it came time to name the business, the choice was easy. Victor wanted his father by his side — not just in name, but on the job. Today, Victor and his father work together with a dedicated crew, bringing the same family values to every property they touch.

What sets Moreno & Son apart is simple: they do the job right. While other companies cut corners and leave homeowners with problems to fix, Victor and his team take the time to listen to what each customer wants, understand their vision, and execute it with care and precision.
